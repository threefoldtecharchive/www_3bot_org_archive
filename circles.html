COMMUNICATION, REDEFINED

Circles revolutionise the way we communicate online.

1

You define your circles

Just like in real life, you create your social groups. This could be family, close friends, not-so-close friends, your project team at work, a volunteer group – the sky is the limit. And just like in real life, the type of information you share or the way you communicate differs from group to group – or circle to circle. With 3Bot, you can be yourself again.

2

Peer-to-peer communication

The way digital communication happens today is ineffective, when you think about it. When you send a message to a friend, this message goes through huge datacenters before being delivered. And in many cases, a copy of that message is made and stored. A bit strange, isn't it? Why would anybody else but you and your intended recipient need to be involved? 3Bots communicate directly between one another, calculating the most efficient and secured route. No intermediaries. No exceptions.

3

Living on the edge 

Today our data is collected and stored by applications and websites in large data centers. With your 3Bot, you choose when and where to store your data. Living on the consious internet, you're now able to store your data on the edge, closest from you, or where you feel its best. Living your digital life on the edge makes it more efficient, safe, private and green. So what are you waiting for? 

circles, unlimited

We naturally organise our lives into circles. We have groups of friends, we have groups of colleagues – and sometimes we have groups of friends who happen to be colleagues as well. With 3Bot, the same becomes possible but in a digital sense. Being at the center, you are able to reorganise your boundaries relentlessly. 

get cosy with your friends

Circles makes it easier than ever before to connect, organise, and exchange with your friends. With circles, it's time to create new memories together. With busy schedules, it can be difficult to get a group together IRL. Let your 3Bot reach out to your friends' 3Bots to figure out the logistics (who, when, where) and you can focus on the fun part. :)

find comfort with your family

Stay in touch with your loved ones by creating your family circle and stay together in reality. With circles, no one is watching you anymore, so you can break free just like at home.

connect with your colleagues

Your work inbox can be a noisy place. How often are we cc'd on emails we don't belong on? Define general and specific work-related circles to ensure the right information is shared with only the relevant people.

discover people around you

It's alway great to meet new people. We enjoy having fun, sharing intimate stories, and learning from each other. And sometimes we move on, but some stay friends. Put yourself out there. Whether by age or interest, it's never been easier to meet new people with circles.

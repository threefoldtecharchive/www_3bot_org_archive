WITH 3BOT, IT'S ALL ABOUT YOU

3Bot is a revolutionary digital avatar that executes your digital needs. It is an unprecedented leap in digital privacy. It comes with all the functionalities you need to interact and exchange on the new Internet. With 3Bot, everything changes.

You're finally in control.

identity

Today, we have many profiles spread across endless applications. With 3Bot, you have only one identity. No need for more.

privacy

You should own your personal data. With 3Bot, not only do you own your data, but you fully decide who gets access to it, and when.

security

Your 3Bot is stateless and peer-to-peer. It is nearly impossible to hack. And 3Bots interact directly, without intermediaries.

assistance & communication

Your 3Bot is completely at your service. It handles your processes and tasks, and allows you to focus on what matters to you.

convenience

We use many applications that work in silos to piece together our digital life. 3Bot gives you access to one integrated digital world.

sustainability

Current storage and communication processes are wasteful and inefficient. 3Bot is a green alternative, living on the edge.

all-in-one access

Our digital lives are scattered across a range of applications and services. Don't you think it's weird to be exposed to countless apps on our devices every day – apps that work in silos, complicating our lives, rather than in conjunction. 3Bot is everything you could want or need, all in one place.

Scroll to learn more.

communication tools

Messaging. Email. Video conferencing. Disjointed communication applications. The tools you know and use on a daily basis – but with 3Bot, they exist together in one location, simplifying your experience. With 3Bot, communication is peer-to-peer, the way it happens in our natural experiences.

information

Knowledge is power. 3Bot will give you access to complementary education tools and services, created to provide people with verifiable and purposeful information. Say goodbye to fake news and articles. Enter a conscious digital world that wants the best information for its people.

complementary banking

Access a digital economy where direct and fair value exchange is enabled and ensured. Tokens are supported by real value – Internet capacity, coffee, an hour of work, or a square meter of rainforest land (for preservation).  And people have access to secured personal wallets to store and exchange instantaneously.

virtual browser

Say goodbye to surfing the web with antivirus, firewall, or VPNs – you don't need these anymore with 3Bot. Just open your private browser and you're two clicks away from all the information in the world, no matter where you are in the world. A browser that does not discriminate you by region.

file manager

Our digital lives are scattered across a range of applications and services with varying levels of security and data privacy. No need for this anymore. 3Bot finds ways to store your files efficiently on the edge, meaning more security, speed, and sustainability, at a lower cost for you.

imagine...

Any application or service you can think of can exist within 3Bot. An extensive, integrated SDK means that 3Bot can grow with the help of developers. We invite you to participate & expand the possibilities of 3Bot. So, let's dream & create solutions to improve the lives of people around the world.
